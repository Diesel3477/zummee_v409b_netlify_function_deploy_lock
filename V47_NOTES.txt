v47 changes:
- assign-vendor-email no longer hard-fails when vendor_name is blank
- function now falls back to:
  1. vendor_name
  2. vendor
  3. assigned_vendor_name
  4. email local-part
  5. 'Vendor'

Why:
The browser payload was reaching the function with a blank vendor_name even though the dropdown
selection looked correct in the UI. This patch makes the email flow resilient.

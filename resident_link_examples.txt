Preferred clean format:
https://zummee.net/workorder/biltmore-parc

https://zummee.net/work_order_submit.html?community_id=YOUR_COMMUNITY_UUID&community_name=Biltmore%20Parc&company_id=YOUR_COMPANY_UUID

Notes:
- For the clean /workorder/<slug> format, your host or router must rewrite that path to work_order_submit.html.
- The page now attempts to resolve the slug against PropertyCommunities by matching a normalized community name.
